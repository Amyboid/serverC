About Us

Our History

Established in 2011 TNU has been a cornerstone of academic achievement and intellectual growth for 20 years. From our humble beginnings to our current status as a leading institution in south kolkata, we have continuously evolved to meet the changing needs of our students and society.

Our Mission

Our mission at TNU is simple yet profound: to empower individuals to reach their full potential through education, research, and community engagement. We strive to foster a culture of lifelong learning, critical thinking, and social responsibility, preparing our students to become leaders and innovators in their chosen fields.

Join us on this journey of discovery, growth, and transformation
